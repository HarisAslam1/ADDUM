Index & Thumb

Thumb DOF

The thumb boasts three degrees of freedom.

Pip Joint

Similar to the human finger, the PIP joint can achieve up to a 120-degree range of motion from its rest position.

Abduction/Adduction

Each finger is capable of abducting and adducting, mimicking the movements of the human hand, with a range of 15 degrees for both actions.

Unique Design

All hand movements are internally controlled.
